## Subtitle

Read Deeper with AI

## Description

Bookgolas helps you track your reading, build better habits, and recall what you've read using AI-powered search.

We've all been there. You finish a great book, but weeks later you can barely remember the key ideas. Bookgolas keeps your highlights and notes searchable, so nothing you read goes to waste.

### Reading Tracker

Organize your books into three simple states: Currently Reading, Finished, and Want to Read. Search and add books quickly, then capture your thoughts with notes and highlights as you go.

### AI Recall

Ask questions like "What did that book say about habits?" and AI will search through your highlights and notes to find the answer. Think of it as a personal search engine for everything you've ever read.

### Reading Goals

Set yearly and monthly reading goals, then track your progress. Watch your completion rate grow as you read consistently throughout the year.

### Charts & Calendar

See when and how much you read with visual charts and a reading calendar. Spot your reading patterns at a glance.

### Reading Insights

AI analyzes your reading habits and gives you a clear picture of your preferences. Discover which genres you gravitate toward and how your reading frequency changes over time.

### Home Widget

Check your current book and reading progress right from your home screen, without opening the app.
